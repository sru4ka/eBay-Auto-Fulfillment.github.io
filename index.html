Shopify App

eBay Auto Fulfillment

A lightweight internal tool to map Shopify variants to eBay listings, review today’s orders, and save the eBay confirmation number back to the order note.

Privacy Policy Terms of Service Contact Support

What data we use Transparency

Orders, Products and (optional) Customers to show daily sales, map items to eBay links, and store your eBay PO number in the order note.

Retention & Deletion

Minimal config only (variant ↔ eBay link, purchase logs). We delete store-identifying data within 30 days of uninstall.

Support

Email us at support@yourdomain.com. We honor GDPR requests via Shopify’s webhooks.
