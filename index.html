How it works

1

Install the app

Grant the needed scopes (Products, Orders, Customers) and open the app from Shopify Admin.

2

Map your variants

Open Variant → eBay Mapping, paste eBay URLs for each variant once. We remember them.

3

Process orders

Go to Today’s Orders. The app auto-saves the eBay order confirmation number to the Shopify order note - no manual copy/paste.

FAQ

What data does the app need?

Products, Orders, and Customers. These are required to show orders, map variants, and save the eBay order confirmation number on Shopify orders.

Do we keep sensitive data?

We store minimal configuration (variant ↔ eBay link) and purchase logs. Store-identifying data is deleted within 30 days of uninstall.

How do I get help?

Email ebayautofulfillment@gmail.com. We usually reply quickly during business hours.
